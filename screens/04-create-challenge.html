Evaluation Rubrics

Rubrics are auto-generated based on your course and selected subjects. They define what constitutes good performance — rubrics are NEVER shown to students.

Framing Rubric
  • Identifies all missing/ambiguous requirements
  • Formulates reasonable and justified assumptions
  • Produces a complete, well-defined task statement
  • Common error: accepting problem at face value without questioning
Judging Rubric
  • Detects logical errors in AI solution
  • Identifies incorrect assumptions made by AI
  • Recognizes incomplete or partial solutions
  • Common error: focusing only on syntax, missing logical flaws
Steering Rubric
  • Commands are specific and actionable
  • Addresses identified issues systematically
  • Converges toward a correct solution
  • Common error: vague commands like "fix it" or "try again"

Rubrics will be regenerated when you change the subject selection.
