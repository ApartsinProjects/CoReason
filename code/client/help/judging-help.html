Phase 2A — Judging

What is Judging? Judging is the skill of critically evaluating AI-generated output to identify errors, gaps, and areas for improvement.

Why It Matters

AI outputs often contain subtle errors. Judging teaches you to:

  • Spot logical errors — Incorrect reasoning or conclusions
  • Identify missing elements — Important aspects the AI overlooked
  • Evaluate completeness — Does the solution address all requirements?
  • Assess correctness — Are the facts and methods accurate?

How It Works

  1. Review the AI-generated solution carefully.
  2. In multiple-choice mode, select all issues you can identify.
  3. In open-ended mode, describe the issues in your own words.
  4. Your judging is evaluated, then you proceed to Steering.

Grading

Your judging is graded A / B / C based on how many real issues you identified and whether you avoided false positives.
