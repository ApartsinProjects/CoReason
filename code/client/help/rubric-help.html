Understanding Rubrics

Each challenge uses three independent rubrics to evaluate your co-reasoning skills.

The Three Skills

Skill What It Measures
Framing How well you structure an ill-defined problem
Judging How well you identify errors in AI output
Steering How effectively you correct AI output

Grade Scale

  • A — Excellent. Comprehensive and accurate.
  • B — Good. Mostly correct with minor gaps.
  • C — Needs improvement. Significant gaps or errors.

Important Notes

  • Each skill is graded independently — you can get an A in Framing and a C in Steering.
  • In practice mode, you see feedback after each submission to learn.
  • In assessment mode, feedback is shown only at the end.
  • Rubrics are generated by AI and are invisible to you during the challenge.
