Understanding Your Analytics

The analytics dashboard shows your performance across all challenges you've completed.

Grade Distribution

The stacked bar chart shows how many A, B, and C grades you've received for each skill:

  • Green (A) — Excellent performance
  • Blue (B) — Good performance
  • Orange (C) — Needs improvement

Trends Over Time

The trend line shows how your performance has changed over time. Look for upward trends — they indicate skill development.

Per-Challenge Reports

Click on any challenge in the list to see a detailed breakdown:

  • Your framing refinements and the AI's evaluation
  • Each judge+steer cycle with grades
  • The AI's final output vs. initial output
  • Suggestions for improvement

For Instructors

Instructor analytics show aggregate data across all students in a course:

  • Per-student grade breakdown
  • Course-wide grade distributions
  • Export to PDF for reporting
