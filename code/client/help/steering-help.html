Phase 2B — Steering

What is Steering? Steering is the skill of writing effective corrections and instructions to improve an AI-generated solution.

Why It Matters

Identifying problems is only half the battle. Steering teaches you to:

  • Write clear corrections — Specific, actionable feedback
  • Prioritize improvements — Focus on the most impactful changes
  • Communicate effectively with AI — Guide without over-prescribing
  • Iterate towards quality — Each cycle should make meaningful progress

How It Works

  1. Based on the issues you identified in Judging, write corrections.
  2. In multiple-choice mode, select the most effective steering commands.
  3. In open-ended mode, write your own correction instructions.
  4. The AI regenerates its solution incorporating your corrections.
  5. A new Judge+Steer cycle begins with the updated output.

Grading

Your steering is graded A / B / C based on how effectively your corrections improved the AI's output.
